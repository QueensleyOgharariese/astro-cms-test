Navier Ellie Trovi is everything an empress should be — poised, intelligent, capable. So when her husband the emperor starts openly flaunting his mistress, she doesn't crumble. She adapts. And the story that follows is one of the most satisfying reads in the entire manhwa landscape.

## Why it's a must-read

The Remarried Empress is rare because it refuses to make its heroine a passive victim. Navier is always three steps ahead, always dignified, always *herself*. Watching her navigate court politics while the people around her underestimate her is genuinely thrilling.

The art is gorgeous — soft, detailed, cinematic. Every panel feels considered.
